about the actor

simba nagpal

Simba Nagpal is an Indian actor and model who predominantly works in Hindi television. He is best known for his debut portrayal of Virat Singh in Colors TV's popular Shakti Astitva Ke Ehsaas Ki. Nagpal also portrayed male protagonist Rishabh Gujral in Colors TV's supernatural franchise Naagin 6. Born: 25 September 1996 (age 26 years), Delhi Movies: Bhayam
