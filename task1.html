Career

Simba Nagpal Article Talk Read Edit View history Tools From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia Simba Nagpal Nagpal in May 2022 Born 25 September 1996 (age 26) Delhi, India Nationality Indian Alma mater Sushant School of Art and Architecture Ansal University Occupations ActorModel Years active 2018–present Known for Bigg Boss 15 Naagin 6 Simba Nagpal (born 25 September 1996)[1] is an Indian actor and model who predominantly works in Hindi television. He is best known for his debut portrayal of Virat Singh in Colors TV's popular Shakti Astitva Ke Ehsaas Ki (2020-21).[2] Nagpal also portrayed male protagonist Rishabh Gujral in Colors TV's supernatural franchise Naagin 6.[3] In 2021, he was seen as contestant on Colors TV's reality show Bigg Boss 15.[4] Life and education Nagpal was born on 25 September 1996 in Delhi, India.[5] He completed his secondary education in Sushant School of Art and Architecture. He later completed his bachelor's in Ansal University, Gurgaon.[6] Career Nagpal made his first appearance in the reality show, MTV Splitsvilla 11 in 2018 as a contestant and ended up at 8th place. In 2019, he auditioned for another reality show, MTV Roadies but didn't qualify the culling round. Following the same year, Nagpal made his acting debut with a leading role as Virat Singh in Colors TV's soap opera Shakti - Astitva Ke Ehsaas Ki opposite Jigyasa Singh in the 16 year leap.[4] After the show ended in 2021, he participated in Colors TV's reality show Bigg Boss 15[7] though he finished at 16th place,[8] but his instinct in the house was praised by host Salman Khan and called him "You're moving ahead with dignity and dignified way".[9][10] In 2022, Nagpal joined Balaji Telefilms thriller supernatural franchise Naagin[11] for its sixth season where he portrayed the male protagonist Rishabh Gujral in Naagin 6 opposite Tejasswi Prakash.[2][4] Initially it received criticism because of story, however later winning hearts especially for the chemistry between lead pairs and gained better ratings itself.[12][13] In December 2022, he quit the show after the leap.

Accolades

Star Eminence Awards 2023: In a stunning turn of events, actor Simba Nagpal has taken home the coveted Best Actor in Indian Television award at the prestigious Star Eminence Awards. The talented actor, known for his exceptional performances, received accolades for his portrayal of a double role in the super natural show Naagin 6, where he played the characters Army Officer Rishabh Gujral and the enigmatic negative character Shakti. Simba Nagpal's win at the Star Eminence Awards comes as no surprise to avid viewers who have witnessed his remarkable acting skills on the small screen. His ability to seamlessly switch between two contrasting characters in Naagin 6 left audiences captivated and enthralled. With his nuanced performances, Simba breathed life into Rishabh Gujral, an Army Officer with a heart of gold, and sent chills down the viewers' spines as the manipulative Shakti.
